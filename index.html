Latest Project

DataPoint

The perfect leads manager for small bussinesses

New

Datapoint is an application designed to help small business owners manage the incoming leads from their business website. Our goal is to notify users of potential clients via push notifications delivered from our Firestore database. The leads will contain information about the client (contact, location) and a small description of the pursued service. Users will be given the option to contact the user (phone, email) with the click of a button and categorize the lead (delete, pending) based on the outcome of the conversation. The project is undergoing development and remains closed source as it will be published in the App Store in the upcoming months.
